About

I am currently in my second year of master's degree in State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems, SCSE, Beihang University. Before that, I received B.S. degree from School of Computer Science and Engineering at Northeastern University in 2017.

I am broadly interested in solving real-word visual understanding problems with optimization/learning-based algorithms. Recently, I am interested in developing learning and optimization frameworks for visual saliency analysis and scene parsing.
